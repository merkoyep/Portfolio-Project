About Marco

Growing up, I had always known that I wanted to pursue a career that made a positive impact. Initially, I thought that becoming a teacher would suit me best because I loved mentoring and coaching others. To pursue this, I went to UBC to pursue a degree in History, and got experience working with children as a program instructor at my local community centre.

It was at City Centre Community Centre that I discovered how impactful community recreation is, and I ended up working full time as a program coordinator for two years, while finishing off my bachelor's degree.

Upon graduation, I was offered a full time position as a Recreation Leader for the City of Richmond. In the next years, I was promoted to a Community Facilities Coordinator position, where I was responsible for a wider range of services and operations at the community centre. One of the key responsibilities I had was integrating a new registration software into our facility's existing operations. From creating user guides to designing workarounds, I eventually discovered an interest in software design.

In 2022, I applied to Shopify's Dev Degree program, and Dominican University of California's Applied Computer Science program. In 2023, I was accepted and am now currently working to develop my skills as a software engineer.
